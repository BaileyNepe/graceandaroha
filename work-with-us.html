Become a Kaiako with us

Values

At the heart of Grace & Aroha and our philosophy are love, care, and respect.

Not only do we strive to respectfully meet the needs of children and whānau with care and love, we believe that the kaiako who work with us deserve to be treated with respect and supported throughout all aspects of their time with us. We know that this is a demanding yet rewarding role and we will be here to provide you with ongoing professional learning and development and will work in partnership with you.

With a consistent philosophy throughout our organisation and in each home, based strongly in Te Whāriki, RIE, and Pikler, the partnership between us and our Kaiako is strengthened as a team working under the same kaupapa.

Some of the support that we offer

  • Help to set up as a self-employed contractor

  • Tax information

  • Teacher registration

  • Professional development

  • Alternative care/Relievers

  • Visiting Teacher Support

How funding benefits you

We believe government funding should be passed on to kaiako to benefit the needs of the children. This is why we pay our Kaiako an additional $2 per child per funded hour These funds are additional to your hourly rate and are intended to be used to establish and update your resource kete.

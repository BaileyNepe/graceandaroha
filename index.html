Aroha mai, aroha atu

With Grace & Aroha, children and their families are cared for through loving, caring, respectful relationships, in order to feel happy, safe and to be able to grow and learn together.

Welcome to Grace & Aroha Educare

At Grace & Aroha Educare, our collective approach to care and learning is indeed grace and aroha; we strive to respectfully meet the needs of children and whānau with care and love, while recognising the potential each child has and supporting their natural growth and development. Children need to be trusted, to lead, play, explore, grow. Whānau should be supported with care as they raise their children. The three underlying principles of our kaupapa are love, care and respect.

Neuroscience tells us that the brain needs to be in a state of well-being before it can be ready for learning. The warm environment of love and genuine connection we foster at Grace & Aroha Educare enhances every child's learning and development.

When we show respect to children through listening and acknowledging them, their thoughts and feelings, they learn their worth and can begin to understand that every person is deserving of respect. Our role-modelling of love, care and respect with our children provides a strong, healthy foundation for them to build all other relationships throughout their life.

We provide quality homely environments where children and whānau are welcomed and supported by dedicated Kaiako.

Through our home-based care and education service, as well as other support services, our goal is to create a community of learners, learning with and from each other.
